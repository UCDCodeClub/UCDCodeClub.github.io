Schedule

UCD_CodeClub meets virtually every 2nd Wednesday at 4pm. A Zoom link will be sent via the mailing list in advance of the session. Students are welcome to join at any time, regardless of their level of experience. Please see below for more details.

Date Time Location Topic
04/11/20 4pm Zoom Introduction to R
18/11/20 4pm Zoom R for Data Science
02/12/20 4pm Zoom R for Data Science

If you would like to join UCD_CodeClub but are unable to attend at these times, or if you have a particular topic you would like to request, please contact UCDCodeClub@gmail.com or fill out this form.

About

UCD_CodeClub is a peer-led support group with the goal of helping UCD students and staff learn the necessary bioinformatics skills to carry out their research in a friendly, supportive environment. We are currently focusing on R, following the ‘R for Data Science’ book by Garrett Grolemund and Hadley Wickham. Join the mailing list or follow for updates.
